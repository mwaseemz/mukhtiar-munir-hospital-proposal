Preamble

This Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) is entered into on this day of ____________, 2025, between Atomic System (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") and Mukhtiar Munir Hospital (hereinafter referred to as "Client"), collectively referred to as "the Parties."

WHEREAS, the Client desires to modernize and digitally transform its healthcare operations through the implementation of a comprehensive Hospital Management System; and

WHEREAS, the Service Provider possesses the technical expertise, resources, and experience to design, develop, and deploy such a digital solution;

NOW, THEREFORE, the Parties agree to collaborate on the Digital Transformation Project under the terms and conditions set forth herein.

Article 1 Purpose & Objectives

The purpose of this MoU is to establish a framework for cooperation between the Parties for the development and implementation of a Hospital Management System that will:

  • Centralize patient data management with secure, real-time access
  • Implement role-based workflow systems for doctors, nurses, surgeons, and administrative staff
  • Provide advanced sorting, filtering, and search capabilities
  • Enable digital signature functionality with multiple design options
  • Enhance operational efficiency and improve patient care quality
  • Ensure data security and compliance with healthcare regulations

Article 2 Scope of Work

2.1 Service Provider Responsibilities

Atomic System agrees to deliver the following services:

  1. Requirements Analysis: Conduct detailed requirement gathering sessions with Client stakeholders
  2. System Design: Create comprehensive system architecture and database schema
  3. Frontend Development: Develop responsive React.js dashboards with TypeScript for all user roles
  4. Backend Development: Build RESTful APIs using Node.js and Express.js
  5. Database Implementation: Design and deploy PostgreSQL database with optimization
  6. Authentication & Security: Implement JWT authentication, role-based access control, and data encryption
  7. Digital Signature Module: Develop custom canvas-based signature generator
  8. Cloud Deployment: Deploy system on AWS or DigitalOcean with SSL configuration
  9. Testing & QA: Perform comprehensive testing and quality assurance
  10. Training & Documentation: Provide user manuals, video tutorials, and staff training

2.2 Client Responsibilities

Mukhtiar Munir Hospital agrees to:

  1. Provide timely access to required information and stakeholders
  2. Designate a project coordinator for communication with Service Provider
  3. Provide necessary infrastructure and resources as mutually agreed
  4. Review and approve deliverables within agreed timeframes
  5. Ensure staff availability for training sessions
  6. Provide feedback and testing support during development phases

Article 3 Technology Stack

The system will be developed using the following proven technologies:

  • Frontend: React.js with TypeScript for robust user interface
  • Backend: Node.js with Express.js for scalable API services
  • Database: PostgreSQL for reliable data management
  • Cloud Infrastructure: AWS or DigitalOcean for hosting and deployment
  • Security: JWT authentication, RBAC, and end-to-end encryption
  • Signature Module: Custom HTML5 Canvas-based generator

Article 4 Project Timeline

The project shall be completed within a period of two (2) weeks from the commencement date, structured as follows:

Week 1: Foundation & Core Development (Days 1-7)

  • Requirements finalization and system architecture design
  • Database schema creation and setup
  • Core backend APIs development
  • Authentication system and role-based access control
  • Initial frontend framework setup

Week 2: Integration, Features & Deployment (Days 8-14)

  • Complete frontend dashboards for all roles
  • Workflow integration and stage management
  • Digital signature feature implementation
  • Advanced sorting and filtering capabilities
  • Comprehensive testing and bug fixes
  • Cloud deployment and staff training

Article 5 Financial Terms

5.1 Project Cost

The total project cost is mutually agreed at PKR 225,000 (Pakistani Rupees Two Hundred Twenty-Five Thousand only).

5.2 Cost Breakdown

  • Frontend Development: PKR 65,000
  • Backend & API Development: PKR 60,000
  • Database Design & Implementation: PKR 30,000
  • Role-based Access & Authentication: PKR 25,000
  • Digital Signature Feature: PKR 20,000
  • Cloud Hosting Setup & Deployment: PKR 10,000
  • Testing & Quality Assurance: PKR 10,000
  • Documentation & Training: PKR 5,000

5.3 Payment Schedule

  1. First Installment (50%): PKR 112,500 upon signing of this MoU
  2. Second Installment (30%): PKR 67,500 upon completion of Week 1 milestones
  3. Final Installment (20%): PKR 45,000 upon successful deployment and acceptance testing
Payment Method: Bank transfer to the Service Provider's designated account. Details to be provided separately.

Article 6 Intellectual Property Rights

Upon full payment of all agreed fees, the Client shall own all intellectual property rights to the custom-developed system, including source code, documentation, and related materials. The Service Provider retains the right to use generic frameworks, libraries, and tools used in the development process.

Article 7 Confidentiality

Both Parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all proprietary information, patient data, business processes, and technical specifications shared during the course of this project. This obligation shall survive the termination of this MoU.

Article 8 Warranty & Support

8.1 Warranty Period

The Service Provider warrants that the delivered system will be free from critical defects for a period of 30 days from the date of deployment. Any bugs or issues identified during this period will be resolved at no additional cost.

8.2 Post-Warranty Support

After the warranty period, ongoing support and maintenance services may be provided under a separate agreement with mutually agreed terms and pricing.

Article 9 Termination

This MoU may be terminated by either Party with written notice of 7 days in case of:

  • Material breach of terms by the other Party
  • Failure to meet agreed milestones without valid justification
  • Force majeure events preventing project completion

In the event of termination, the Client shall pay for all work completed up to the termination date based on milestone achievements.

Article 10 Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this MoU shall first be attempted to be resolved through mutual discussion and negotiation. If unresolved, the Parties agree to seek mediation before pursuing legal action. The applicable laws of Pakistan shall govern this MoU.

Article 11 Miscellaneous

11.1 Amendment

This MoU may be amended or modified only through written agreement signed by both Parties.

11.2 Entire Agreement

This MoU constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties and supersedes all prior discussions, negotiations, or agreements.

11.3 Severability

If any provision of this MoU is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
